## Severity Guide

- Critical: Blocks task completion or causes major failure.
- High: Causes significant confusion or repeated errors.
- Medium: Slows users down or creates uncertainty.
- Low: Minor friction or polish issue.

## Review Checklist

- Are all issues backed by observed behavior?
- Are recommendations realistic for the product team?
- Are severity levels reasonable?
- Are user quotes anonymized?
- Are next steps clear?
