# Responsible AI UX Checklist

Use this checklist when designing or reviewing AI-assisted product experiences.

## User Value

- Does the AI feature solve a real user problem?
- Is AI necessary, or would a simpler interaction work better?
- Does the feature reduce effort without reducing user control?

## Transparency

- Is it clear when the user is interacting with AI?
- Are AI-generated suggestions labeled appropriately?
- Can users understand why a suggestion was made?

## Control

- Can users edit, reject, or undo AI-generated outputs?
- Is there a clear fallback when AI output is not useful?
- Are users able to complete the task without relying fully on AI?

## Privacy

- Is sensitive data avoided or protected?
- Are users informed before their input is processed?
- Is personally identifiable information removed from examples and logs?

## Quality

- Are AI outputs reviewed before being used in important decisions?
- Does the product handle uncertainty or low-confidence outputs?
- Are harmful, biased, or misleading outputs considered in testing?

## Team Workflow

- Who reviews AI-generated outputs?
- How are issues reported and improved?
- How will the team monitor misuse or unexpected behavior?
