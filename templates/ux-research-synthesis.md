## AI-Assisted Synthesis Prompt

```text
You are helping synthesize UX research notes. Identify recurring themes, user pain points, user goals, and product opportunities. Keep the analysis grounded in the notes. Do not invent findings. Separate observations from recommendations.

```

## Output Review Checklist

- Are the themes supported by the research notes?
- Did the AI invent anything unsupported?
- Are sensitive details removed?
- Are recommendations clearly separated from observations?
- Can the product team act on the findings?
